About Me

Hi, I’m Shuvam Mohanty — a marketing enthusiast and brand-builder in the making. With a background in engineering and hands-on experience in branding, digital marketing, and creative strategy, I help ideas find their voice and connect with real people.
I recently worked as a Project Coordinator at Double Tap, where I collaborated on marketing and branding campaigns for clients across real estate, retail, education, and sports. From developing campaign strategies to creating digital assets using tools like Canva, Figma, Google Workspace, and Meta Ads, I contributed directly to brand storytelling and execution.
I’m passionate about making things that matter — whether it's shaping brand identities, writing compelling copy, or crafting marketing strategies that actually stick. My work sits at the intersection of design thinking, content creation, and digital communication.
Currently, I’m building my personal brand and sharing weekly thoughts on marketing, creativity, and storytelling across Twitter and LinkedIn.
🎯 Mission: Turn ideas into brands. Stories into strategy. Creativity into real-world impact
